Welcome To Bungusland is an animated musical comedy series following the adventures of Billy Bungus and his girlfriend who is a Sea Cucumber. Billy Bungus is an aspiring chef who, after inheriting an old stone tower, begins a secret double life as Mayor of a strange place called Bungusland which he travels to by flushing himself down the drain! We join Billy each episode as he encounters all sorts of bizarre situations and fun, unique characters while struggling to deal with his dual responsibilities. Welcome to Bungusland…an exciting world of hilarious songs & endless adventure…

Billy Bungus

Billy is the central figure and his girlfriend is a sea cucumber that he carries around with him.

He is a normal teenage boy who is kind, overly trusting and aspires to be a chef in the restaurant in which he works.

His catchphrase is ‘Whaaaerd!’ which he exclaims in extreme situations.

He can be a little dim-witted at times.

Sea Cucumber Girl

Sea Cucumber Girl is Billy’s girlfriend and sidekick.

She is very smart and the real brains of the operation solving difficult situations in her own quiet way, often without anyone noticing.

She speaks using little noises.

She secretes a purple liquid called Bungus Juice which is delicious and Billy’s secret ingredient in his aspiring chef career.

Crazy Professor And Bionic Crab

He is a mischievous character whose plans to dominate Bungusland nearly always backfire.

The Crazy Professor is driven by a desire to become mayor of Bungusland and a resentment of its inhabitants. He is Billy’s arch nemesis.

He is intelligent but impatient and slips into rages when things don’t go quite as planned, which is often.

He manufactures a drink made of  Sea Cucumber Juice. His bumbling henchman is a ballet  loving Bionic Crab.

Prawn Paul

Prawn Paul is a rapping prawn. He is up-beat and positive. He is a close trusted friend of Billy and Sea Cucumber Girl.

Lobsquitch

The Lobsquitch is part lobster, part squid and part ostrich, a Godzilla sized wanderer who lives in Bungusland.

Emily Epiglottis And Gwretchin

Emily is Billy’s friend. She is an energetic & lively tomboy who has a romance loving epiglottis called Gwretchin that pops out from her mouth.

Gastronaut

He is an overweight astronaut who is friendly & well-meaning, often caught between his desire to help and his search for a snack.

Tony Dourbain

The arrogant head Chef at the restaurant in which Billy works. He is always on Billy’s back, suspicious of his strange absences.

Robot Walrus

Created by the Crazy Professor, he is a gigantic robotic walrus that always turnsup at the worst moment to cause havoc.

Aunt Betty

She lives in Bungus Tower. Billy doesn’t realize that she is the Crazy Professors Grandmother orchestrating mayhem in Bungusland.

Captain Climate Change

When he’s in Bungusland Billy has an over-zealous, eco-obsessed super hero alter-ego.
